On A Personal Note...

Before diving into the world of software I worked as a chef for a very long time.
I started cooking professionally when I was 14 years old and went to school for my Red Seal Certification which I finished in 2017. Since then I have been working as a chef. I made the decision to leave the industry and pursue a career in software development as I need better work-life balance for both my mental health and to have more time with my wife and puppy.

My personal hobbies and lifestyle is very out-doors driven which is in part why I love Victoria and have been living here my whole life. My biggest hobby and passion is rock climbing, it is something that takes up a large majority of my free time and I try to spend most of my summers outside at the many local crags and mountains. I also enjoy hiking, cycling, and training calisthenics most of which is a supplimental workout to help improve my climbing, I love to push myself both physically and mentally and tend to always give 100% in whatever I do.
